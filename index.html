LOCATION

Ominous Descent

1910 Hwy 60, Bartow, FL 33830

FROM TAMPA

Get on I-275 N from N Florida Ave
  1. Head west on E Zack St toward N Marion St
  2. Turn right onto N Florida Ave
  3. Turn right onto E Scott St
  4. Turn left onto the Interstate 275 N ramp to Ocala/Interstate 4 E/Orlando
  5. Keep right at the fork, follow signs for Interstate 4 E and merge onto I-275 N

Follow I-4 E. Take exit 25 from I-4 E
  1. Merge onto I-275 N
  2. Use the right lanes to take exit 45B for I-4 E toward Orlando
  3. Continue onto I-4 E
  4. Keep right to stay on I-4 E
  5. Take exit 25 toward County Line Rd

Follow County Line Rd and FL-60 E to Main St W in Bartow
  1. Turn right onto County Line Rd
  2. Turn left onto FL-60 E
  3. Make a U-turn
  4. Destination will be on the right

FROM ORLANDO

Get on I-4 W from W South St
  1. Head east on W South St toward S Orange Ave
  2. Turn right onto S Orange Ave
  3. Turn right at the 1st cross street onto W Anderson St
  4. Turn right onto Boone Ave
  5. Turn left onto W South St
  6. Turn left to merge onto I-4 W

Follow I-4 W and US-27 S to FL-544 W
  1. Merge onto I-4 W
  2. Keep left to stay on I-4 W
  3. Keep left to stay on I-4 W
  4. Keep left to stay on I-4 W
  5. Use the right 2 lanes to take exit 55 to merge onto US-27 S

Continue on FL-544 W to Winter Haven
  1. Turn right onto FL-544 W
  2. Continue onto 1st St N
  3. Continue onto E Lake Silver Dr NE
  4. Continue onto 1st St N
  5. Turn right onto Cypress Gardens Blvd

Take US-17 S to Main St W in Bartow
  1. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto 3rd St SW
  2. Continue onto US-17 S/Eloise St SW
  3. Turn right onto US-98 N
  4. Continue onto W Van Fleet Dr
  5. Continue onto Main St W

WARNING

WARNING: Entering Ominous Descent Haunted Attraction entails known and unanticipated risks that may result in physical and emotional injury. Risks may include: slipping, falling, collision with fixed objects or other participants, bruises, twists, sprains, breaks, seizures, and possibly death.

DO NOT ENTER a haunted house if you suffer from asthma, heart conditions, are prone to seizures, physical ailments, have respiratory or any type of medical problem, are pregnant, or suffer any form of mental disease, including claustrophobia. By reading this warning, you understand that such risks are to be expected when entering any haunted attraction, including Ominous Descent. These risks cannot be eliminated without jeopardizing the essential qualities of this activity.

By purchasing a ticket to Ominous Descent Haunted Attraction it is expressly understood that you have been made aware of these risks and you agree to assume all the risks existing in this activity. Your participation is purely voluntary and you elect to participate in spite of the risks.
